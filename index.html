How it works

From address to answer
in four steps.

No engineering degree required. Just answer the prompts.

01 --
Enter the address

Type in the zip code. JLoadPro instantly pulls ASHRAE design temps, wet bulb, and climate zone automatically.

02 --
Describe the building

Walk through a guided form: construction type, insulation, window specs, square footage. Dropdowns pre-loaded with common assemblies.

03 --
Review the loads

See sensible and latent cooling, heating load, equipment size, and room-by-room CFM - all live as you type.

04 --
Export the report

Download a complete permit-ready PDF with your company logo and all inputs documented.

AR photo measurement

Snap a photo.
Skip the tape measure.

Point your phone at any room, window, laser measure display, floor plan, blueprint, or hand-drawn sketch. JLoadPro reads the dimensions automatically and fills the field. No more squinting at tape measures or re-entering numbers from a notepad.

Rooms and floor plans
Photograph a floor plan, blueprint, or hand-drawn sketch. AI reads the room dimensions and calculates square footage instantly.
Windows and doors
Snap the window frame or the label on the glass. JLoadPro extracts rough opening dimensions and adds them to the correct orientation automatically.
Laser measure displays
Got a Bosch, Leica, or Fluke laser? Point your camera at the screen - the reading transfers straight into the field, no typing required.
